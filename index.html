Bright Spark Engineering consistently delivers impressive performance

Energypac Engineering, a Bangladeshi multinational based in Dhaka, operates across the technology, engineering, construction, power, and energy sectors. With over four decades of experience, we are Bangladesh's premier provider of power products and systems, as well as the leading turnkey solution specialist for supercritical power transmission and distribution projects. Our expertise is demonstrated by the successful completion of more than 500 projects in the 33kV, 132kV, 230kV, and 400kV ranges.

Who We Are ?

Founded in 2024 under the leadership of Director and CEO Engr. AL -Amin Hossain The company has an impressive history of completing over 500 turnkey substation projects within the 33kV, 132kV, 230kV, and 400kV voltage ranges, consistently driving innovation in the industry. This commitment to advancement is exemplified by the creation of Bangladesh's largest power transformer, a custom-designed 230/132kV, 300 MVA Autotransformer, developed to fulfill the requirements of the Power Grid Company of Bangladesh.

What We Do .

Energypac Engineering Limited offers end-to-end electrical engineering solutions with a diverse product range, including transformers, switchgear panels, busbar trunking systems, and fireproof electrical equipment for residential, commercial, and industrial applications. Their portfolio covers power generation, transmission, and distribution systems, featuring advanced products like GIS, ACRs, and AVRs. Beyond manufacturing, Energypac excels as a turnkey substation provider, earning a reputation as a dependable partner in the electrical engineering sector. The company's innovative solutions cater to a wide array of industries and client needs.

Our Achievements

Awards and Certifications: Energypac Engineering Limited has received the National Export Trophy five times for excellence in the electrical and electronics sector. Their ISO-certified facilities and the only ISO 17025-certified power lab in Bangladesh ensure top-quality products that meet international standards like IEC, BS, and ANSI/IEEE.

Global Impact: The company has completed landmark projects, including powering Bangladesh's Rooppur Nuclear Power Plant, supplying systems for the Metro Rail project, and delivering high-voltage substations in Nepal and India. Energypac exports to over 15 countries, solidifying its global presence.
